FAQ
Do I need a website or any tech skills? +
Not at all. If you can fill a Google Form, you can set up your menu. We handle everything — hosting, the link, the QR code. You just add your dishes.
How does WhatsApp ordering work? +
When a customer taps "Order via WhatsApp", a pre-filled message is sent to your WhatsApp number with their order details — items, quantities, and their address. No API setup needed on your side.
Can I update my menu daily? +
Yes! You can toggle any item as "unavailable" or "sold out" in seconds. Perfect for home kitchens where stock changes every day.
What's the difference from Swiggy/Zomato? +
Swiggy and Zomato take 25–30% of every order, own your customer data, and control your visibility. With Menufast, you keep 100% of every order, own your customer list, and your menu works on your terms.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. No lock-in, no cancellation fee. If you cancel, your menu stays active until the end of the billing period.
